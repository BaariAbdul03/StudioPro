# 🎨 Visual Identity

## Core Palette
The StudioPro palette is built on a deep charcoal foundation with high-contrast electric accents, optimized for professional, long-session creative work.

- **Brand Primary:** `#2e62ff` (Electric Blue) - Used for primary actions, active states, and brand highlights.
- **Surface (Background):** `#0b1326` (Midnight Navy) - The base background color for the entire application.
- **Surface Container:** `#121b2e` - Used for secondary panels, cards, and grouped content.
- **Surface Bright:** `#1a243d` - Hover states and tertiary elevations.
- **Outline:** `#2d3a54` - Subtle borders and separators to maintain structural clarity without bloat.
- **On Surface (Text):** `#ffffff` - Primary headings and body text.
- **On Surface Variant:** `#94a3b8` - Secondary labels, descriptions, and metadata.

## ✍️ Typography
We utilize **Geist** (Grotesque) for its technical precision and modern character.

- **Display (XL):** 48px / 1.1 leading / Bold - Page headers and hero text.
- **Headline (LG):** 24px / 1.2 leading / Semibold - Section headers.
- **Title (MD):** 18px / 1.4 leading / Medium - Card titles and panel headers.
- **Label (SM):** 13px / 1.5 leading / Medium - Navigation items, button text, and property labels.
- **Mono (XS):** 12px / 1.5 leading / Regular - Data values, SKU numbers, and code snippets.

## 📐 Material & Spacing
A consistent geometric rhythm ensures the app feels "engineered" and professional.

- **Border Radius:** `8px` (Standard) for containers and panels; `4px` (Sharp) for buttons and inputs.
- **Spacing Scale:** 4px base (4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64).
- **Glassmorphism:** `backdrop-blur: 12px` with `background: rgba(18, 27, 46, 0.7)` for overlays and floating panels.

## 🧱 Component Patterns
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Electric Blue background, white text, 4px radius.
  - Ghost: Transparent background, Outline border, On Surface Variant text.
- **Navigation:** Full-width top bar, centered/left-aligned links, 0px border-bottom with Outline color.
- **Inputs:** Surface Bright background, 1px Outline border, 4px radius, Label SM typography.
